A New Department


Washington University has re-established its Department of Sociology to focus intellectual inquiry on central social issues that affect modern US and global societies.

The first year of faculty recruiting was a stunning success with the appointment of three distinguished sociologists who are now on campus. We are actively recruiting new junior colleagues to join the faculty in the fall of 2016.

Explore this site for more information about our rapidly growing programs.

As Dean of the Arts & Sciences Faculty, Barbara Schaal, has said: “Sociology is an essential academic discipline that investigates important issues of human social structure and function — issues that are at the heart of many national and global challenges. The new department will enhance our ability to educate our students and conduct world-class research in areas that are central to the critical social issues of our time."
