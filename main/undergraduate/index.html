About the Undergraduate Program

The Department of Sociology began to offer undergraduate courses in the fall semester of 2015. The curriculum continues to expand with many new offerings planned for the 2016-17 academic year.

Plans to create major and minor programs are underway in the spring of 2016. We expect that students will be able to declare a major in sociology at some point during the 2016-17 academic year. Check back here for further information.
